Discover the main sights of Toronto to plan your tours and not miss a thing. Capital of the Province of Ontario, Canada's largest city is one of the darlings among tourists who choose the country as a destination. Half the population of Toronto is coming from other countries. Thus, several neighborhoods have emerged, such as Chinatown, Little Italy, Little Portugal, with several establishments that highlight the culture of the respective countries, as well as a very hospitable people, who welcome the tourists with a lot of sympathy.
